Whalers on the moon bit from futurama

link to source

This is one of my favorite bits from the show. The cast travels to an amusement park on the moon and goes on a dopey ride that shows what historians in the future think life on the moon may have been like. This is a funny social commentary on how we percieve history as absolute facts, but oftentimes we may be wrong.
